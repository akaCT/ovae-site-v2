03 Why it's happening

Three reasons. All yours to fix. None of them are "post more."

/ 01

Your site points at Brea

Your most important pages — the main HVAC, electrical, and water-heater pages — are titled and headlined for Brea, not Orange. The pages with the most weight are telling Google "this is a Brea business." So that's where you rank.

#3–#4 in Brea · invisible in Orange
/ 02

Your Orange pages are orphaned

You actually built the right pages — "plumber in Orange," "AC repair in Orange." But nothing on the site links to them, so they get no authority and can't rank. The correct pages exist and are starving.

Built · linked from nothing
/ 03

Google can't tell who you are

Across the web your business shows 5–6 different phone numbers and two addresses, with duplicate Yelp/BBB listings and a likely-wrong primary category. When Google can't resolve one clean identity, it stops trusting you in the pack.

1 business, 6 phone numbers, 2 cities
