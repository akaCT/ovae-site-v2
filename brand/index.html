01 — Essence

The seed of an owned intelligence.

Ovae — from the Latin ova: the origin, the seed, the beginning. The brand is built on a single idea: Ovae is the owned operating layer a business grows from. Not a tool it rents. A core it keeps.

Everything visual serves that idea — and serves a market saturated with loud, gradient-soaked "AI" branding by doing the opposite. Ovae is calm where others shout, owned where others rent, and quietly alive where others sit static. It should feel less like a SaaS dashboard and more like a well-set book that breathes.

Personality

Considered

Editorial calm. Narrow measures, generous space, nothing rushed. Confidence shown through restraint.

Owned

Grounded, durable, plain-spoken. We don't hype — we state the real number.

Alive

A perpetual, subtle motion layer. The page breathes; it is never busy.

Warm & precise

Warm paper ink and film grain against a dark, exact, teal-lit system.

The north star

A printed editorial spread fused with a calm dark instrument — warm paper texture in the dark. If a choice feels loud, decorative, or generic, it's wrong. One accent. One idea. Made beautifully.

02 — The Mark

The Ensō.

One tapered brushstroke forming an egg — the ova, the origin — open at the top. Drawn, not drafted. A gesture, not a glyph.

What it means

  • The egg — ova, the Latin root of the name: origin, the seed a business grows from. The owned core.
  • The single stroke — one human gesture, ink loading through the base and drying at the tip. Made by hand, run by a system: exactly what Ovae sells.
  • The opening — at the top, where the stroke begins thin and ends tapered. Room to ascend; an origin, not a closed loop.
  • The dot is not here — by design. It lives in the wordmark, as the period of Ovae. One dot per composition (see §03).

Construction

A single closed path on a 100-unit grid — bounds x 13–88, y 10–94, optical center (50.5, 52). The stroke starts thin at top-left, loads to a belly of ≈13 units through the left and base, and tapers to nothing at top-right. Opening ≈ 40°, centered just right of 12 o'clock. Never redraw by eye — use the canonical path from the logo kit.

Clearspace & reduction

The tapered single mass holds its character down to 16px with no simplified variant needed. Below that, don't shrink the mark — use the wordmark; its teal period is the brand's smallest atom.

03 — Wordmark & Lockups

The wordmark.

Set in DM Sans Medium with the defining trailing period — Ovae. — a full stop that says: this is the system of record.

Horizontal lockup
Ovae.
Stacked lockup
Ovae.
Wordmark only — the period is the dot
Ovae.

The Deloitte move: the brand's dot lives in the name as the teal period. Use wordmark-only in body copy, signatures, and tight UI. The legacy dot-prefix is retired.

The one-dot rule

One dot per composition. In lockups the only dot is the wordmark's period — never add one beside the mark. Spacing: gap between mark and wordmark = the stroke's belly width; the mark's optical center aligns to the wordmark's x-height.

07 — Iconography

Thin, geometric, line-drawn.

The checkmark is the brand's recurring glyph. Icons are SVG line-work — never emoji, never stock.

Stroke 2.2 at 24px, round caps and joins. Match the mark's weight family.

08 — Motion

Alive at rest.

Motion is ambient, slow, and purposeful. The system breathes; it never demands attention — except the single CTA nudge.

Breathing mark
Button sheen

Easing & timing

/* standard */ --ease: cubic-bezier(.4,0,.2,1); /* premium entrance / choreography */ --ease-out: cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1); /* springy pops */ --ease-spring: cubic-bezier(.34,1.56,.64,1); /* micro .15–.22s · state .3–.4s · fills .55s · never > .7s */

All ambient motion is gated behind prefers-reduced-motion. Numbers count up; nothing flashes.

09 — Texture & Imagery

Warm paper in the dark.

A near-invisible film grain over everything, plus faint radial glows. This is the texture that makes the dark feel analog, not digital.

Film grain · opacity .04 · overlay

An SVG feTurbulence fractal noise, warm-tinted, fixed over the viewport. It's on this very page. Keep it on every full surface.

Ambient glows

Two low-alpha radials — teal top-right, rust bottom-left — imply light without color noise. Imagery stays sparse, calm, warm-in-dark; never busy stock photography.

10 — Voice

Calm, precise, never hyped.

We sound like a plain-spoken expert. Short sentences. The real number, not the adjective. Accuracy is our differentiation.

✓ Say

"Most AI projects stall. We're the ones who finish."

"You've got $1.5–3M trapped in 60–90 day receivables. We recover it."

✕ Don't say

"We leverage AI to unlock transformative synergies."

"Revolutionize your business with our second brain."

Banned words

transformation · leverage AI · unlock · revolutionize · second brain — the hype words everyone else uses. We don't.
